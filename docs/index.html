Introduction

If you are tired of voting for a candidate and then to realize that once elected that he/she does not hold the promises and engagements he/she defended during the campaign, the following political system might interest you.

To avoid such pitfall, we should thus not elect persons, but just the "ideas" the candidates pretend they would defend. The following is a proposal of such system and as a proposal is presented for feedback, comments and adaptations.

For brevity the political system hereafter described will be referred as Government By Ideas or in short GBI.

About the Author

The author is a simple citizen in France, employee in the private sector and not linked with any political party nor part of any lobby. He presents his work as a proposal and request for comments and has conscience that this is a work in progress.

Concept

The GBI aim is to provide an enhancement to democracy that puts the people closer to decisions and get them interested to the decision process, having them more frequently involved than once every 4 to 5 years. As a side effect it should avoiding lobbies to decide in the place of people, thus lead the fight against corruption and putting back the state global interest at the center of the government actions.

Scope

GBI is not a substitute for justice, for police, for education, for healthcare, nor any other regalian services.

GBI's target is to have laws modified, created and withdrawn in order to adapt them to new realities a country is facing. It thus takes the role of a legislative system and also partially of an executive system. But GBI has not the role to enforce laws for what stays in the hands of police and justice.

As shortly explained previously, in addition to be a legislative system, GBI is a partial executive system. Why partial? Because GBI decision process need time and is not adapted to actions that must be taken quickly like defense, war, fight against terrorism and any other new situation that may arise putting the country at risk *and* needing fast decision. But action that engages the country regarding other countries (treaties, public foreign affairs) must not be excluded from GBI decision system. For that reason this is only a partial executive power where a president function is still needed and will have to be elected as per democracy usage.

Risk: risk is that such president or a group of person behind him take over the GBI by invoking imminent danger (like mass destruction weapons against us) to take action that else would be under the scope of GBI

Elections Process

Ideas election

GBI means to not elect candidates but to elect ideas. Ideas have first to be presented and defined. This first step is open to any citizen without restriction, this is the idea not the author of the idea that is important at this step.

But to avoid the system to be overloaded under ideas, each submission should cost a symbolic fixed amount of money plus an amount proportional to the revenue of the submitter. Such amount should allow any working citizen even the ones with the smallest legal revenue to pay the submission of one idea per year and still having enough money to satisfy the minimum level of expenses required to live with dignity. The number of idea a person can present per year should probably not to be limited as we might have very good contributors as well also as very poor ones. But we will see that poor or defective ideas will just be ignored and will not cost much resource to anyone except its author.

Once an idea has been submitted to the system it receives an Idea ID that identifies it and will have to be voted for. An idea will be retained if a majority of registered voter approves it. Abstention is considered the same as rejecting an idea, but explicitly voting against an idea is possible and has some interest which will be detailed later. In consequence voting for a given idea shall not be mandatory.

Risk: here the risk is that no idea would get enough vote for the country to pass new laws in order to adapt to new needs. This is the way that forcibly lead people to be concerned by the life of their country for their problems to get solved. To be representative any idea cannot be adopted if less or equal than 50% of the electors have voted for it, this clause is fundamental for GBI. However we can also precise who and how a citizen can be an elector, this is decribed in a specific paragraph.

The risk stays however that people could vote on orders or for retribution, or delegate their power to a given organism, this is however not a new risk compared to current democracy systems, but the pressure on voices acquisition could be stronger with GBI and thus should be looked at and penalized.

Limiting the number of idea per person and per time is not necessary as they will just be ignored if their submitter has either not put the means to make it known, understood and accepted, or if the submitted idea is just silly, useless, not interesting. No effort will be required to have it been purged from the GBI system.

The delay between voting and idea submission, is the time during which the idea is exposed to debate. The submitter will have to define this duration when submitting the idea and choose it in a way to maximize the number of votes and still not delay the idea too much in the future for it can be taken by electors as a solution to the current problems.

This is thus the duty of an idea submitter to make it known, understood, voted for and accepted. All mean are possible for that, including advertisement, social networks, emails... Debates could take place anywhere, usenet newsgroups, public meetings, emails, social media, whatever, but always clearly indicating the idea ID's they debate about, compare, valuate, promote or criticize. However unfair communication means that do not allow anyone to present its idea (TV broadcast, newspaper...) in particular when privately owned, should not be allowed to mention exposed idea and should be fined in case they would refer directly (Idea ID) or indirectly (title, tag, keywords) to an idea.

Risk: the reason why to forbid unfair communication means to promote ideas is to reduce the risk of citizen brain washing (TV, newspaper...) by owners of such communication means, omitting to mention alternatives to ideas not in phase with their own ideology. This is thus a way to reduce the mean a lobby can have to influence people voting.

Once the exposition delay has been passed, the vote for an idea are counted and either the idea is adopted (if it has a majority of votes) or it is rejected (if not). There is no need to synchronize the time and date of votes between different ideas.

Risk: the strongest risk here is that GBI strongly need computer science based systems. Especially for voting, unlike physical vote where any citizen can check that there is no fraud in each vote center, and seen the publicly available result of each vote center checking that the global vote is true (but how many citizen take the time to do that today?), using computer science means completely removes the citizen control against fraud. A voting system is proposed in annex that leverage Internet access and tend to minimize fraud.

The fact voter can change their mind and the nature of their vote during the exposition period of an idea is interesting and should be possible (thanks to strong authentication system, see the proposal for a such system below).

It could also be defined that when the time remaining before end of vote is only a third of the total exposition time (for example), voter could not change their vote anymore, only elector not having yet voted could add their voice to or against the concerned idea.

having the information of vote number for ideas currently available for vote could be a possible option. In addition providing a top list of the most voted idea could be interesting to compensate the 50 % minimum requested electors giving their voice for an idea to be adopted.

Super-Ideas

An idea can be dependent on another one. Each idea will thus have a dependency list (eventually void), but cyclic dependency (idea A depends on idea B which itself depends on idea A) should not be allowed. We will design by super-idea such idea that relies on at least another idea.

A new idea could thus list a set of other existing ideas (by their idea's ID) and voting for it would imply voting for all ideas it depends on. Of course an elector could not vote for an idea if this one has in its dependency list an idea the voter has already explicitly voted against. However the elector can still vote for any other idea presented in the dependency list of this super-idea, but not for the super-idea itself. This way an elector could vote against some ideas and see what's left to vote for.

A super-idea could also be listed in the dependency list of another idea. This way, a given idea could be referred in the dependency list of many other ideas, directly or indirectly, making a tree of ideas.

In addition to that, an idea could also be referred by several different super idea trees making common branches between these trees.

We could imagine political party proving their own super-ideas and their set of dependent ideas. We could also imagine some other political party borrowing some sub-ideas from a first party by adding them in the dependency list of their master super-idea (directly or not). Though any citizen could decide to vote for any leaf-idea whatever is the party or citizen it has been submitted by as well as for super-ideas that have this leaf-idea in their dependency list, leading the ideas common to different political party to have more chance to be elected.

Minister

Now that an idea has been retained thanks to the idea election process described above, a person has to be selected (not elected, but randomly chosen - see chapter for that topic) to implement it. Any people that has the right to vote and has not been condemned for corruption in their live can postulate for a given idea. The selected candidate will be called the minister for this idea. A person cannot be minister for two different ideas at the same time. But a person can be minister in his life as many times as he has postulated and has been selected.

The role of the minister will be to implement the idea, that's it to define all the necessary for this idea to become applicable in the perimeter described by this idea submitter. He will also have the duty to regularly provide action status: what has been done so far, what stays to be done, what are the problem met today to go forward, and so on. The frequency of these action status are defined by the author's idea.

If a super-idea has been elected, a minister for this super-idea will have the duty to implement the whole tree of dependent ideas (see Ministerial Cabinet below). There will not be minister selection for any of the sub-idea of this super-idea.

However if two elected super-ideas have the same idea in their dependency list, or depend on the same branch of ideas (a common branch of ideas between two elected trees), this sub-idea or this branch of ideas they depend on will have a dedicated minister and will be removed from the responsibility of the super-idea ministers. If any such super-idea need first thus sub-idea or sub-branch to be implemented in order to start, it will be delayed. This is the minister decision to define when the implementation work for an idea starts, is suspended and ends. He will also have to justify his decision as defined by the idea submitter at least during the planned action status reports.

Censors

In parallel to minister candidates, censors have to be selected (randomly chosen from the list of censor candidates) for a given idea to control that the actions status provided by the minister are true and sincere. Censor could trigger Justice if there is significant difference between actions status report done by the minister and reality of facts, or if the action performed would be outside the perimeter of the original idea. A minister could be this way revoked and condemned. He would not be allowed anymore to postulate for a role of minister for the rest of his life. If the judgment does not retain the minister to be guilty, the sensor should not be prosecuted, unless he triggered justice without strong enough reasons to do so. In that case he may be condemned for censorship abuse. A censor could be condemned up to three time for abuse in his life (being censor for the same idea or for another one), after that he won't be able to be selected as censors for the rest of his life. No other penalty should be applied in case of censorship abuse.

Risk: if censors do not feel free to investigate and if necessary, to trigger the Justice, the risk is that this role will serve no purpose. However a mechanism must exist to prevent abuse. The fact the sensor is randomly chosen for an idea reduce the risk but does not remove it completely that a set of persons consider their role of censor in regard of the selected minister not in regard to the action he performs in regard to the idea definition and expectations (opposite/different political party or human conflict), or worse in regard to the nature of the idea itself (idea the censor does not like would receive a lot of "investigation" against the minister, or an idea the censor approves would receive little or no control for its execution by the minister...).

If a super-idea has been elected, there will not be a super-censors, but as many censor as there is ideas and sub-ideas. This rule will make sense with minister cabinet described below. The resource censorship must not be reduced for the level of control to be independent from the super-idea hierarchy and selected minister(s) to implement them.

Note: An alternative to having a censor per idea is to define professionals/officials independent from the number of ideas. The history showed that such public human resources tend to be reduced over time in the expenses reduction plans. The consequences would be a lack of control on the execution of elected idea and probably the same drawback as current system, where a (s)elected person does not execute the expected actions (implementing another idea than the one people have voted for).

Elector

During discussions, some underlined that many people today (probably the majority of voter) are and will never fill concerned by the political activity of their country. This would make the corum of 50% an idea should have to be retained difficult or impossible to reach. To cope with this, being counted as an elector could imply to actively register as such. A small fee could be set for this demand (no more than 1/1000 of the minimum legal salary for example), the only cause of refuse could be the same reasons an citizen today cannot be an elector (age, conviction for corruption,...).

Once a citizen is registered as elector, he will have the right to vote but also the duty to be an active elector. This means to perform a minimum number of actions per registration period (1 year for example) not to become forfeited of his voting right. In that case the citizen would not more have the right to vote, propose idea or postulate for a role, but could register again paying a majorated fee (twice the fee of what was payed at the previous registration for example). The action that could count a citizen as an active elector and thus that would avoid him to be forfeited of his voting right, could be of course the fact to vote for or against an idea, the fact to be candidate for a role of minister, the fact of being part of the ministerial cabinet, being president, having proposed ideas that have been retained, and so on. The minimum number of votes (if nothing else qualifies the eector as active) could be defined based on what a working citizen can reasonably do on his personal time, like voting for at least 100 ideas per month. Which means for example, voting for a super-idea that depends on 99 other idea or subscribing to a "tag" (see political party below) which associated organism proceeds to the voting of at least 100 ideas per month). Of course, if a voter is unable to vote for health reasons of force majeure and can justify is situation for a give period of time, the minimum number of required votes to stay an elector will be reduced pro rata temporis.

What must contain an Idea

An idea is not just an idea, it must define means that will be provided to implement it

Objective

The objective describes the idea by itself. For example "reduce the maximum authorized speed for motorized vehicles to 110 km/h on motorways" is an objective. However this ideas implies defining penalties to driver that would be caught above the speed, providing mean to indicate the maximum speed, measure the impact of the speed reduction on pollution, people health, economy and so on.

The objective of a super-idea must be coherent with its child ideas.

Risk: is that one person or an organism could hides in the idea-tree some ideas that implement the opposite of the super-idea, leading people to be trumped. A penalty system against idea submitter would be difficult to be defined. Though the fact the idea submitter and the minister are different persons should reduce such abuse. In that case if the minister can demonstrate that the super-idea he has to implement lists ideas that tend to propose the opposite action of the super-idea, he can be granted to not implement this sub-idea. The minister will issue a idea extension (see below) to redefine the perimeter of the super idea explaining why he wants to remove certain sub-idea(s). The elector that voted for this idea will be notified of this idea extension and will have to vote for or against this extension.

Time span

An idea must define a maximum duration of execution, it must also define the frequency of action status reports the minister would have to provide. Once the maximum duration has passed the minister ends his role whatever is the status of the idea. After that time the implementation evaluation process starts.

Budget

Each idea should provide a budget for its implementation. A separated budget (compensation budget) would define the amount of money that the minister and his team (see Ministerial Cabinet below) will be allocated for the whole time span of this idea. An amount will also be allocated for sensors. An idea submitter cannot reduce the amount redeeming the censors below a minimum level, it can however define a higher amount. At the opposite, the compensation budget for minister can be zero, but maybe then no candidate will postulate for such idea as interesting as it can be for the society.

A super-idea can have his own budget, but it must also list the sum of all budgets of the child ideas recursively for an elector to see the global cost of what he can vote for or against.

Perimeter

The idea should define a perimeter outside which the action should not apply (in our example this might be that wind or human powered vehicles should not be concerned by this speed limitation).

Idea ID

Last an Idea ID is attributed by the system once the idea has been duly registered. Such ID must be chosen for the distance between two IDs differs from at least three characters (a typo of one or two characters should not lead to a valid ID). For the same reason, only either the O letter or 0 digit should be used inside idea identifiers.

Ministerial Cabinet

A minister may appoint other persons (experts, specialists,...) in order to get help addressing his goal. He will then share with them the compensation budget equally during their time of presence, these persons will constitute the Ministerial Cabinet. The minister has all power to modify his cabinet at any time. The budget will be shared prorata temporis of presence in the Ministerial Cabinet.

Risk: a minister may delegate his role to a so called expert that will bypass the random selection system of candidates. In the other hand a minister cannot be hampered in its action for the pretext he engaged a expert that takes over his role... Puppet politician already exist today, how to reduce this risk here?

Ideas extension

It may arise that some idea get almost realized but not completely and having the same minister and ministerial cabinet to completely achieve them would make more sense that performing again the whole process of idea election. In that situation, an idea-extension can be submitted by the minister. All voter for the original idea will get notified by such idea extension, but still a majority of electors will have to vote for it for the extension to be granted. Elector that explicitly voted against such idea will also be notified.

Idea extension may concern time span extension and/or budget extension and/or perimeter extension. It will have to be justified in order for voter to understand and agree with it. The censor may amend or annotate the explanation of the minister, he may also trigger the justice if the extension differs too much from the original idea. The exposition delay is, here too, defined by the idea submitter (here the minister), to find a good balance between the remaining time to realize the idea and the necessary time to obtain the necessary vote quorum. If elected, such idea extension does not lead to candidate selection, this is the minister that will implement it.

Idea extension can also be used by the minister when there is conflict between a sub-idea and a super-idea or between ideas of a tree he has the charge to implement. This may lead to the withdrawal of a sub-idea that has an opposite goal of the super idea people have been voted for.

End of execution

An minister and associated sensors ends their role once the maximum duration time for the idea has ended. Minister or censor may also resign in which case they will have to refund the money they received for their role, unless major force (decease, diseases duly validated,...)

the elector that have been voting for this idea get notified and can provide a mark on the way the idea has been executed. This notation will increase or decrease the minister selection weight which will either reduce its chance to be selected or increase its chance to be selected as minister if he postulates for a minister role in the future.

Tax System

Citizens

The budget sum of all ideas accepted during a given year is added to the tax amount that will be applied to citizen the next year. An idea that span several years will have a proportional fraction of its budget allocated each year. Each idea may define the way its budget will be allocated over time (for example 80 % the first year, and 5 % the four next years).

The way to balance this taxation between people (what we will call taxation model) based or not on their revenue, patrimony, age, volunteer in general interest cause... is outside the scope of the GBI.

When voting for an idea, voters will see the cost they will have to pay for this idea, in regard to the existing taxation model and their position in that taxation model.

It should be provisioned enough money to address the budget in regard to past years expenses and currently running ideas. If provision is not large enough, firstly voted ideas of the year will be active, the other will stay pending for budget or at the will of the minister will start without fund or with privately owned funds, reimbursed later to the minister. The state has not to carry any interest for the loan if it could not provide the necessary fund for the idea to be active. A pending idea is one that has been elected but not started and its described time span is shifted up to the time budget will be available.

Risk: the lack of provision may lead the idea submissions to take place at a given time of the year to have more chance to get a budget sooner, leading to race between ideas in regard to the time schedule to have its budget allocated sooner.

Companies

As companies have no right to vote for ideas, they shall not be taxed to setup the budget of any idea. However companies do consume resources (natural and human resources) as well as public services (transportation, police, justice...) that the society provides to them, they should be taxed regarding the use they do of natural and human resources in particular about the cost of resource recycling, and for public services in regard of the use they do if it.

Natural Resources

For natural resources, a company will be taxed proportionally to the recycling cost of the product they build/transform. This implies that the company provides information about the transformation applied to the natural resources or already transformed resources used as base of their production, in order to estimate the cost of recycling.

Risk: rely on suppliers to do the biggest ugly part of resource transformation, for that reason, importations should also be taxed in regard to the cost of recycling the product will have.

Following the same principle as VAT, a "RAT" for Recycling Added Tax, could address this cost, usually left to the society by bankrupted companies. By natural resource, it must also be take into account the surface used for buildings and the reference for such tax is the recycling cost to get back to pre-human activity status (status the land had before the human even have used it): the cost of removing all building, concrete, roads, pollution, and so on.

The value of an agricultural field when no chemical product have been used (for example) during decades and soil pollution is absent can be considered equivalent (thus maximal) to pre-human activity status. Forests that are managed for long-term can also be assimilated to this highest level of value as there is no recycling cost: just wait the plant to grow to get back to pre-human status equivalent. This is not the case for a ruined building as one can wait for plants to grow, once trees will get their maximum height the building and the traces of human activities will still be visible, and/or will still have measurable consequences on flora and fauna if soil pollution occurred, for example.

In consequence, a company that recycles resources will get compensated accordingly to the cost the society would have taxed a company doing the opposite action. For example a company which action is to recycle trash will get paid according the difference of value from the trash and the resulting recycled product.

Adding the recycling cost to product manufacturing should lead companies to select the best raw materials and best transformation process that ease recycling. And if not, the society will not have to cleanup the mess while company just enriched with beautifully ugly products for the environment (plastics, ...)

Human Resources

For human resource, recycling means maintaining employability for employees by mean of training, and/or role change. What should count in this evaluation is not the salary, but the difference of employability each employee has when entering the company and when leaving it.

The state should compute yearly statistics for a meaningful set of role categories and provide for each role:

  • The average unemployment duration, referred as UNEMP in the following
  • The average employment duration, referred as EMP
  • The average annual salary, referred SAL in the following

We can then define the Employability Ratio (ER) for a role category by:

ER(role) = EMP / (UNEMP + EMP)

This is the average fraction of time a person having this role is employed

The difference of employability when the employee enters and leaves a company gives the variation of the fraction of time an employee will stay unemployed. The cost to the company is the Employability Tax (ET) which is defined by the product of Employability ratio variation times the average unemployment times duration times the average salary for the role employee had:

ET = [ ER(entrance role) - ER(exit role) ] * UNEMP(exit role) * SAL(exit role)

This can mean the company will receive money for the ET if the employee has increased its employability on the market:

But of course this also means that if a company does not take care of maintaining employability of its employees, it will have to pay a tax according to the decrease of ER:

It means a company does not carry the load of unemployment for a given employee that leaves the company, but only the degradation of value an employee had on the market by staying in the company. And if the ER value increased during the time the employee stood in the company, this one would be reward for that.

But, to avoid a company to artificially change the role of employees before firing them and this way avoid paying for the ET, during the average duration time of the new role a penalty ER is introduced which lead the ET to be the following:

  • First the ET reward expressed above should be prorata temporis of the employee time in this new role compared to the average employee duration for that role.
  • Second we introduce the notion of Penalty ER, which corresponds to the ER the employee had N years before his role change, where N is the duration the employee has spent in its new role (see diagram below). The difference of ER compared to the entrance ER times gives a second ET that is added to the first ET prorata temporis to the complement of the first ET time ratio:

That way just after the change of role the benefit of the role change is little because the time spent in this new role is little (prorata temporis). At the same time the Penalty ER is the one the employee has in his former role just before changing of role, thus the penalty ET is maximum because the time ratio for penalty is also maximum. This is as if the employee had left without having changed of role. As time passes and as the employee has more experience in this new role, the benefit ER increases and the penalty ER decreases both because the Penalty ER decreases in value but also because the time ratio for it is reduced as time passes. After EMP time in the new role, the time ratio for the penalty ET is null and the ET is equal to the ET calculated previously only considering the entrance ER and the exit ER (see examples below)

It is assumed the indemnity provided to unemployed people is proportional to the average salary for the role the employee had in his previous professional experience (and not linked to its real own salary). The cost of unemployment is charged on all salaries of active persons, sort of mandatory insurance for unemployment. As a consequence the unemployment cost is paid by the citizen only, the company pays only the loss of value of their employee when they leave, this should lead company to provide training to their people that have real value on the market for their employee and also promote them when a given job is decreasing.

Why not making difference when an employee resign and when he is fired? Because it may leave due to pressure from the employer.

Why not making difference when an employee resigns for a new job? Because it does not change the need for a company to maintaining employability. This does not forbid company to contract conditions (if conform to law) for the employee to engage for a given period of time in the company for the new role.

Last the ET should be calculated each year and redeemed or charged to companies each year, as if all their employee were leaving at the end of year and were arrived at the end of the previous year. This way it takes care of company bankruptcy.

Example 1:

Mr Doe has been employed for 10 years in company A always in the same role category, which at the time he entered the company had the following indicators: UNEMP = 1 year, EMP = 5 years, SAL = 60 k€. After these 10 years, Mr Doe leaves or is fired by company A, but the role indicators are now: UEMP = 2 years, EMP = 5 years, SAL 50 k€
The ER at entrance was: 5 / (5 + 1) = 0.83
The ER at exit is: 5 / (5 + 2) = 0.71
The ET for enterprise A is: (0.83 - 0.71) * 2 * 50 k€ = +12 k€

But if the indicator for this role had been better (unemployment duration reduced for example) company A would have received money accordingly, without having done anything!

We see that the unemployment indemnity for the former employee will be 100 k€ in average (for two years), this is not the company that pays this amount, company only pays the variation of cost the unemployment for this role has between the time the employee has been engaged and the time the employee leaves the company.

Example 2:

Mr Doe has started at company B as role 1 (UEMP = 1, EMP = 3, SAL = 50 k€) after 5 years, role 1 indicators are (UEMP = 2, EMP = 3, SAL = 45 k€) at that time company B to avoid the corresponding ET cost decides to promote Mr Doe to role 2 with a salary of 50 k€ (no salary change) eventually providing him an adapted training for this new role . After 6 years in this role 2, which we assume has not changed significantly over years (UEMP = 1, EMP = 6, SAL = 70 k€) Mr Doe resigned for another company.
The ER at entrance was: 3 / (3 + 1) = 0.75
The ER at exit is: 6 / (6 + 1) = 0.85

As Mr. Doe has had an experience duration greater or equal the EMP in his last role (6 years), the penalty ER is ignored and the company is rewarded the following ET:
ET = (0.75 - 0.85) * 1 * 70 k&euro$ = -7 k€

But if Mr Doe had left or was fired after one year instead of 6, the penalty ER would have played:
Penalty ER = 3/(3 + 2) = 0.6
The ET would have been:
ET = ET1 + ET2
ET1 = (0.75 - 0.85) * 1 * 70 k€ * 1/6 = -1,16 k€
ET2 = (0.75 - 0.6) * 2 * 45 k€ * 5/6 = +11,25 k€

Where from the total ET = +10 k€

Company B would have to pay 10 k€ for the Employability Tax. This mechanism avoid the company to try reducing the ET by changing the role category of employee just before firing them. The fact that Mr Doe only stood one year while the average employment duration is 6 years also means this experience at role 2 is too short to have a complete value and Mr. Doe may experiment difficulties being engaged under this role in the future.

Costs of Risks

If an employee dies due to risk inherent to the role he has or due to lack of security addressed by the company, this one will have to redeem the heirs to the last salary the employee had for the time remaining to his retirement. Any membership fee for retirement shall be performed as if the person was still alive and would provide a retired amount up to the average life expectancy for the role the employee had. Proving the origin of the injuries would be a decision done by the Justice (nothing automatic here are their are ways to abuse the system by employee as well as employers)

For that Reason Company should have insurance for any risk they can bring to the resources, whether human or natural. In particular an Industrial company that manipulate dangerous chemical substance that could explode or pollute the grounds should contract an insurance in regard to this risks and this way be able to fix and compensate any catastrophe which could be generated by its activity.

Use of public Services

Companies should also be taxed in regard to their use of transportation infrastructure, common and public services, as well as for regalian services. If for the first ones the tax can be proportional to the use (transportation, postal services when publicly owned...) care should be taken for the access to Justice to be possible even for little or emerging companies. The tax could be for example indexed on the turnover that each company had the previous year (no tax for the first year of exercise of a company). Note that losing a pursuit does not mean having no cost for such small companies. In other words, each company will have to pay a tax for justice which amount will depend on their turnover whatever they are suing or not.

Controlling system

The cost of controlling (real use of natural and human resource, adequate risks insurance subscribed and proper declaration of public services usage) must be carried by the companies' activities. This cost can be balanced between companies taking into account each company's turnover, here again. The fine amount in case of infraction should be chose high enough to compensate the lack of risk to be caught by the controlling system. Less tax implies less means for the controlling system resources, thus the fine amount in case of fraud must be increased in consequence for dissuasion: in other words, the product of (the fine amount) x (the frequency of controls or difficulty to be caught) must be constant.

Constitution

Once an idea has been retained but before a minister get selected, it must be validated for conformance with the constitution. A constitutional council is needed for that and can be solicited by the president of the executive power, president of the justice power and by some associations recognized of social interest (like association acting against corruption, for human rights...).

The constitutional council can also give a status without external solicitation. In case of rejection of an idea, the constitutional council must justify its decision based on the constitution. If an idea is rejected by this council this will lead it to the same fate as if it was not elected.

The definition, of the constitution is outside the scope of GBI, though one could allow the GBI system to add, modify or remove rules of the constitution, it should be done with some additional validation process outside the perimeter of this proposal (higher ratio of voter for this constitutional idea...)

Executive Power - President Role and interaction

As stated in introduction, the GBI in addition to be a complete legislative power is also a partial executive power that needs to be completed by a presidential role as of today in many democracies (directly or indirectly elected by people). The frontier between actions that the president can take and those that he cannot take alone should be defined precisely and is out of the scope of this proposal. However, some decision the president has the power to take alone could still have the form of a presidential idea with maybe smaller quorum of elector necessary for its adoption (relative majority of voter for example, with a amount of votes corresponding to at least 30 % registered electors). Also a dedicated budget could be defined in advanced for such situation and needed actions. Such idea will be implemented the same as other ideas: by candidate selection not by the president or by its cabinet.

In another hand some actions and responsibilities should not be under the hand of the GBI system like (nuclear power, weapon use, war, etc.) and would be taken without passing through the GBI process.

Referendum

Referendum are just a particular case of submitting a idea to the GBI system. Electors as well as the executive power can propose idea. But only presidential ideas can lead to a mandatory voting from all electors.

Execution Conflicts

It may arise that two elected ideas enter in conflict. For example, a first elected idea would tends to increase security of car drivers in case of accident by introducing a geolocation automated devices in vehicles that would detect the occurrence of an accident and trigger emergency alert, while another elected idea (possibly already completed) would for example control the personal information collected from people including their geolocation all along the time.

For that reason a set of prime ministers has to be selected (randomly from a list of candidates and for a fixed duration) to arbitrate between ideas and define the frontier between ideas when two or more collide.

Instead of one single person, having an odd number of prime ministers randomly selected in a list of candidates (same criteria as for minister to postulate to this role) would lead to reduce the risk of arbitrary decision.

The prime ministers action should only take place when a minister claims a conflict against another rule. They should modify each idea perimeter in order for the conflict to disappear. Only 3 prime minister will be randomly selected to arbitrate a conflict at a time. But a broader set of prime-minister can be selected in regard to the expected load of arbitration they will have to address.

During prime-minister resolution conflict, concerned ideas are suspended, this means their time span is extended, but their budget stays the same.

Risk: There is the risk a party provides under his master super-idea a set of useless ideas that could be used to trigger a conflict with other ideas that they are against. This could undermine idea with little compensation budget due to the fact the minister would not be compensated during the conflict resolution.

Random selection

As described above, minister, sensors and prime-ministers would be randomly chosen from a list of candidates. We also saw that postulating to a role has some constraints:

  • one cannot postulate to a minister role, if it has even been condemned for corruption
  • one cannot postulate to the role of censor if it has been judged three time for censorship abuse
  • one cannot postulate to the role of prime-minister if it has been deprived of his rights of elector, condemned for corruption or for censorship abuse.
For all candidate roles, we could also add some constraint like to not be in prison, and so on.

Now we should also valuable successful role execution, by mean of weights. Random selection could be weighted in the following manner:

  • A minister that has successfully implemented an idea (and this introduce the notion of notation at the end of idea execution by the electors that voted for that idea), could have his weight multiplied by the mark he would have received (using 1 as initial weight). This will lead him more chance to implement another idea if he postulate for a new minister role in the future (in the other way, the mark of a minister condemned for corruption would become zero, forbidding any further selection)
  • A censor that could demonstrate the corruption or result falsification of a minister and lead him condemned could have his weight multiplied by 10.
  • A censor that would have be condemned for censorship abuse would have its weight divided by 5 for each such sentence received (up to 3 in his life).
  • But as it is difficult to validate the way a prime minister has performed, there would not be weight for this candidate role.
    • If in a candidate list a first candidate has a weight of 10 and a second has a weight of 1 the probability for this later to be selected would be 1/11 and for the first 10/11. Chances is that the list of candidate would be much greater, if not the weighting value could be revised.

Doing that way, leverages candidates with good skills for a given role. Professional politician could well emerge but should be sanctioned the same as newcomers and be banned forever from the role if they failed (condemned for corruption, abuse...) even if their weight was very high over time.

Any candidate for an election as well as any citizen should be able to assist to the selection process. It could even be broadcasted to public media. Each selection must be performed under the control of a bailiff using a mechanical machine (not computer science based here, as there is no way to control from the outside the fairness of the random selection). This requires physical presence of the bailiff to assist to the random selection and a set of official mecanical machines, controlled regularly for they fairness (see loto system for example). There could be every day a batch of candidate selections for their corresponding set of ideas, maybe even several bailiff in parallel doing that selection or different sets of ideas. Public announcements would be done some days before about the concerned selections, in order for people to be able to control its execution flow. In particular the candidates could be notified of their date/time of selection.

Return to civil life

Care must be taken for ministers once they have completed their idea to be reinserted into civil life. For that reason during a time proportional to the idea they has achieved with success (thus, this does not concern condemned ministers), they will benefit of a reserve corresponding to twice the lowest legal salary times the duration of their work as minister (this reserve is independent from the idea compensation budget and ideas global budget) and multiplied by the mark they would receive at the end of execution in such a way an average performance would be rewarded the lowest legal salary while the highest performance would be rewarded twice the lowest salary.

This reserve can be used by the former minister at his discretion but he will never get more than twice the minimum legal salary per month. The goal is both to face unemployment and motivate the citizen to candidate and succeed in their role of minister.

Risk: a balance has to be defined between the level of mean provided to get back to civil life and avoiding people postulating to this role just for the money they will have during the role and the compensation they will have after to carry their return to civil life. Care must also be taken for the global cost of this feature as there may be a lot of minister in a given period of time...

Other member of the ministerial cabinet are not concerned by such mechanism as they were not chosen randomly, but for their skills. As such they did not had to abandon their work and can add this experience as expert or specialist on their resume. Their experience as expert for a member of the ministerial cabinet is the continuity of their day to day work and if they were successful they would have more chance than before to find a job, including a new role of expert in another ministerial cabinet.

Political Party

By political party we mean here any group of persons sharing a common view and/or ideology, this may well be also a philosophical group, or any common group of interest or company. Such entity can register a "tag" to the GBI system which they will be allowed to add to any existing idea, whatever is their author. Tags can be either positive or negative, meaning either that the corresponding political party approves or rejects such idea. Of course such political party would tag positively the ideas they submit with their own tag, but some of their super idea might well rely on already existing ideas and it would not make sense to duplicate an idea as it would reduce its chance to be elected. Idea borrowing that way is thus strongly encouraged by listing them as sub-idea of a super-idea or by adding tag to it to flag them to electors as conform to the ideology of the political party. An idea can thus receive several tags from several political parties. The tagging of an idea requires authentication to avoid tag usurpation. A negative tag can be set instead to an idea that is rejected by a given party.

For elector, such tag system may help validate the impact and consequence of an idea if they trusted the group of person that own the tag (association, political party, philosophical community, and so on). This is also the way a political party could give directive to its members. An elector could of course rely on several tags and vote for idea that match positive tags of a list of parties unless it also matches negative tags of another set of political parties.

We could also imagine a system where elector can subscribe to a given set of tags define a maximum budget and have the system voting for the ideas having at least one of the selected tags (and not negative tags from another defined set of tags) when its cost is below an elector defined amount and while the global elector defined budget has not been exhausted.

Risk: This mechanism may lead some elector to completely delegate their voice and not becoming concerned anymore in the future of the country, just complaining about what they have in their plate, or rather what they have not in their own plate, ignoring the rest of the society. However they will vote for idea, thus in a way the would participate in the decision process of their country.

Idea submitter consideration

It could be added as an option that idea submitter get rewarded when an idea they submitted has been elected. The reward could be financial and visible as such in the total cost of the idea. This amount could even be defined by the idea submitter inside a limit range fraction of the total budget of the idea. At least the cost of the idea submission should be return to the submitter if this idea has been elected.

Conclusion

The objective of such political decision system defining a new legislative system and partially redefining the executive system is to involve people closer to the decision process and keeping a eye on the way it is executed. There is many options (automatic tag voting) that can let people just live they political life as of today, but at least for more concerned citizen, there is room to propose, room to choose, room to control and room to act, while traditional political party would still have their place in that system.

Having people interested in the life of their country, in their own future would make more difficult for a lobby to pass some laws for the advantage of a very minority or not being hold as responsible for the pollution or injuries they caused. If all eyes look at a basket of gold, thieves have to wait for a distracting event to have a chance to grab unnoticed some pieces of the fortune. However if nobody looks at the basket, this one will become empty very soon and nobody will even have an idea of who were the thieves where the went with our fortune, this is the situation we can have today in politics when people do not get concerned by the politician actions.

My strongest hope is that such political system or another leads as soon as possible for decisions to be taken and action to be performed in regard to the global warming and more generally for the general interest of the civilization not only for the one of a rich subset of people. The Drake equation mention a lot a factor that lead to estimate the number of extraterrestrial civilization we should be able to communicate with, but it can also be applied to our own civilization. in particular, its last factor, "L", defines the civilization lifetime and this is the one we are playing with now. Unfortunately if we do not take action now (if we just negate the precautionary principle, for example), this value for our civilization might well be very very short. But after all, maybe our civilization is not adapted to surviving and will perish by natural selection?

Annexes

Voting System Example

GBI implies frequent election for many submitted ideas, relying on new technology is necessary to reduce cost for the society and time for the citizen. However care must be taken to provide vote privacy and control. The following is a proposal of system that is in fact independent on the GBI but is provided just to illustrate the feasibility of the frequent voting requirements of GBI.

For that purpose two independent organism can be defined:

  • Elector Identity Bureau - EIB, which registers electors and authenticate them
  • Virtual Assembly - VA which contains exposed and adopted ideas, search engine and voting system.

EIB

This bureau can be a centralized or distributed organism, each city for example registering its citizen based on official passport or ID card and a legal register of person banned from the GBI system for corruption or other justified reasons. Its first role is to attribute an Elector ID to each electors. An Elector ID a public number with integrated checksum (to detect typo error). An Elector is also given a secret pin/password/passphrase, which the elector is the only one to know (like PIN for sim cards, credit cards, and the like). EIB may renew ID from time to time and will communicate the new ID by postal letter as of today or ciphered emails if the elector has registered a GPG key and email to the EIB portal.

EIB provides an SSL portal to the electors on Internet that has several purposes:

  • Register a GPG public key and email (after having authenticated with the Elector ID)
  • Hide the IP address of the elector terminal to the VA, hide the identity of the elector to the VA
  • Provide a single sign on for the VA
  • Place to report fraud about Elector ID usage
The EIB is thus the only organism having the correspondence between an Elector ID at a given time and the elector person it has been assigned to at that time. In particular the VA does only know the Elector ID and has no information about the Elector itself.

A terminal used for voting by an elector (laptop, smartphone, tablet...), when connecting to the VA, has to use the EIB portal as web proxy. The VA portal must *not* be accessible directly from Internet, but a page of information should be telling how to configure the Elector terminal to vote in total privacy. VA access is ciphered by SSL thus EIB has no way to know about the vote done by the elector when the communication is established from the terminal to VA through the EIB Proxy. At initial connection and for a limited time, the Elector first authenticate to the EIB proxy which generates a token the terminal will use during the session with VA. The VA will receive this token and interrogate the EIB on its validity and Elector ID correspondence).

When the user will perform a vote on the VA, the VA will provide the token and Elector ID to the EIB, which will validate the authenticity of the Elector to the VA. The fact the session passes through the EIB proxy hides from the VA the original IP address and enforce anonymization of Electors to the VA.

VA

The Virtual Assembly is the place where is gathered and registered the ideas. This registration and payment is done without interacting with the EIB, submitter must be publicly identified and be registered. This is the VA idea submitter portal

The VA is also the place where --- through the EIB proxy and with EIB authentication --- votes take place. Once authenticated to the VA voter portal each elector has a view of exposed ideas including the ones he has already voted for (or explicitly against), He can also see the ideas that are currently executed (having information about minister and ministerial cabinet, sensors and budget used, the action status report provided by the minister, and so on), and has the possibility for the VA voter portal do not display (forgets) the ideas he has voted for or against (he can reactivate it afterward but wont see votes done before that time).

Elector should have the ability to search ideas by keywords, by end of exposing date (date at which nobody can vote for it and its vote count is evaluated), by authors, by tags and combination of those filters. The possibility to browse ideas up and down in a tree of super-idea and sub-ideas should also be necessary (when a super-idea contains sub-ideas the Elector rejected, he may want to vote manually for some other sub-idea except that one.

Of course if an Elector has voted against an idea, some color code should show that status for such ideas but also for the super ideas that due to that vote cannot be voted for. Same thing when voting for a super-idea all sub-idea should have color code telling the Elector has voted for implicitly. View should also allow an Elector to see not yet voted ideas only, voted for ideas or voted against ideas.

A set of tags and their value (positive/negative) could be combined to define color code of idea votable for the elector and non votable for the elector (not conform to the set of tags the elector has defined).

The VA voter portal is only accessible through the EIB proxy, to hide IP source and enforce anonymization of the voter. Once an idea has been voted for, some statistics about the number of votes are sent back to the EIB for control:

When a voter votes for an idea, EIB get solicited for Elector authentication and can count the number of votes a given Elector has done but EIB cannot know for which idea the elector has voted.

EIB can thus during a given period of time compare the total number of votes summing the number of votes per electors with the total number of votes received from VA statistics by summing the amount of voter for each idea that has been voted for during that time. This should make fraud a bit more difficult.

Securing Elector Terminals

The terminal or user device has to be secured. Choice must be left to the elector to define the security of his terminal by itself or to rely on vendor terminal certified as secure by the EIB or even provided by EIB. EIB should also provide good practice for a secure Election process, including social engineering menace and all that goes with (phising, etc.)

Risk: is that EIB by mean of such terminal provided by itself get information of the votes done by the elector and even modifies votes done without the elector noticing.

Structural Costs

The cost of such legislative system (idea election) and executive system (minister, censors and prime-minister) is contained in the budget of the elected idea. This is thus the electors that define their level of tax and accordingly their level of services. Majority being the decision maker, we should reduce the case where a small subset of the population (a lobby or a company) passes laws that only benefit to this minority.

Remains for the state some structural costs:

  • All regalian services, as of today
  • Constitutional council members, as of today
  • Prime ministers, which are not compensated from an elected idea as they have to arbitrate perimeters and priorities between them, no representation role, less budget need but more people
  • The voting and idea submission system (and its controlling entity)
  • The candidate selection system (and its controlling entity)
  • A super censorship that may be elected by the people to control the censors not to have collusion with the minister they share an idea with.
